### 1. COMMERCE DISCOVERY ENGINE
**Auto-aggregate products from 20+ sources** (Amazon, Shopify, ClickBank, SaaS, AI tools, courses)
- Users discover: what to buy, sell, promote, use, and what business to start
- Multi-source product display with price comparison
- 5M+ products by month 2

### 2. MONEY-MAKING OS
**First destination for "How can I make money online?"**
- Affiliate opportunities + side hustles + dropshipping + digital products
- Creator monetization + SaaS ideas + freelance work
- Intelligent opportunity matching by user interests

### 3. CREATOR + SELLER MARKETPLACE
**Empowered creators and sellers monetize directly**
- Upload digital products, AI prompts, templates, courses, services
- Custom store pages + seller dashboards + analytics + payouts
- Seller tiers with increasing benefits/commission reductions

### 4. AI RECOMMENDATION ENGINE
**Intelligent ranking that learns and improves**
- Predict CTR, conversions, and user intent
- Personalized homepages, recommendations, search
- Trend detection + forecasting
- Real-time latency: <100ms

### 5. REVENUE INFRASTRUCTURE
**6+ monetization layers, each reaching $1M+ ARR by Year 3**
- Seller commissions (40% revenue)
- Sponsored placements (15%)
- Premium subscriptions (20%)
- Data/APIs (10%)
- Enterprise solutions (10%)
- Advertising + Other (5%)

FineZ will NOT rely on a single revenue stream. Instead, we build 6+ revenue sources that each reach $1M ARR by Year 3.

## REVENUE STREAM 1: SELLER COMMISSIONS (40% of Year 1 revenue)

### Commission Structure
```
DIGITAL PRODUCTS (Software, Templates, Prompts, Courses)
├─ Tier 1 (0-$1K/month): 15% commission
├─ Tier 2 ($1K-$10K/month): 12% commission
├─ Tier 3 ($10K+/month): 10% commission
└─ Enterprise (negotiated): 5-8% commission

PHYSICAL PRODUCTS / MARKETPLACE
├─ Tier 1 (0-$5K/month): 10% commission
├─ Tier 2 ($5K-$50K/month): 8% commission
├─ Tier 3 ($50K+/month): 5% commission
└─ Enterprise: Custom terms

AFFILIATE LISTINGS
├─ If FineZ promotes product: 5% of FineZ commission
└─ Passive income for sellers

SERVICES (Consultation, Design, etc)
├─ Tier 1: 20% commission
├─ Tier 2: 15% commission
└─ Enterprise: 10% commission

DROPSHIPPING
├─ Per-order: $1-5 platform fee
└─ Plus optional analytics subscription
```

## CUSTOMER ACQUISITION COST (CAC) & LIFETIME VALUE (LTV)

### Seller LTV

**Assumptions**:
- Average seller lifetime: 2 years
- Average seller GMV: $5,000/month
- Platform commission: 8% average
- Monthly revenue per seller: $400

**Calculation**:
```
LTV = Monthly Revenue × Gross Margin × Lifetime (months)
LTV = $400 × 90% × 24 = $8,640 per seller

With acquisition costs of $50-100/seller, payback period = 1.5 months
```

### Creator LTV

**Assumptions**:
- Average creator lifetime: 1.5 years
- Mix: 95% free, 4% Pro ($9), 1% Pro Max ($99)
- Blended ARPU: $0.50/creator/month

**Calculation**:
```
LTV = $0.50 × 95% gross margin × 18 months = $8.55 per creator

With acquisition costs of $1/creator, payback = 1 week
```
